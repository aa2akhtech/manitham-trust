About Us

Manitham Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting communities through education, healthcare, and social welfare programs. Our mission is rooted in empathy, equity, and action.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide sustainable development opportunities for marginalized communities.

Vision: A compassionate world where every individual lives with dignity and purpose.

Our Initiatives

  • Free Education Camps – Supporting rural students with resources and mentoring.
  • Health Camps – Organizing free health check-ups and awareness drives.
  • Women Empowerment – Vocational training and support for women-led businesses.
  • Environmental Action – Tree plantation and cleanliness drives.
